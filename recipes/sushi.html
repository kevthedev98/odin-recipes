Sushi Recipe

This homemade sushi recipe includes a variety of fresh ingredients to make delicious sushi rolls and nigiri right at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Fresh salmon, tuna, and shrimp for nigiri
  • Nori sheets
  • Avocado, cucumber, and carrot for rolls
  • Soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi for serving

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice: Rinse the sushi rice thoroughly. Cook with water, then season with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt once cooked.
  2. Prepare the fillings: Slice the fresh fish, avocado, cucumber, and carrot into thin strips.
  3. Make the sushi rolls: Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat, spread a thin layer of rice, add the fillings, and roll tightly. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Make the nigiri: Shape small mounds of rice and top each with a slice of fresh fish.
  5. Serve: Arrange the sushi on a platter with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.
